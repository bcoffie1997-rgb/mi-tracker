# Nationwide APEX Accelerator Contact List & Outreach Pipeline (May 2026)

## TL;DR
The APEX Accelerator network (formerly PTAC) comprises a nationwide network of over 90 APEX Accelerators with over 300 local offices and more than 600 procurement professionals (per the DoD National APEX Day release of October 8, 2025), coordinated nationally by the National APEX Accelerator Alliance (NAPEX), whose first Executive Director is Darryl Thomas (former Illinois state director).

This report compiles state-level lead directors, host orgs, emails/phones, websites, and LinkedIn URLs for every state APEX program verifiable from public sources (napex.us locator + state program sites), and flags high-priority outreach moments including Idaho's host-org transition, the May-2025 leadership change at Maine APEX, the August-2024 promotion of Empire APEX's Lenora Leasure, and Montana's apparent interim-director status.

Coverage: ~52 distinct state/territory programs identified; ~30 with verified director email; ~14 with confirmed LinkedIn profile.

## Key Findings

- **Network structure:** Per DoD's Oct 8 2025 National APEX Day release — over 90 APEX Accelerators, 300+ local offices, 600+ procurement professionals. Served 56,000+ businesses, $24B+ in 2024 contracts. Rebranded from PTAC to APEX Accelerator after the move from DLA to DoD OSBP on October 1, 2022.
- **NAPEX leadership:** President Frank Dixon (USVI), President-Elect Joey Scott (Ohio), Past President Jodi Essex (Iowa), VP Lenora Leasure (Empire/NY), Treasurer James Gerraughty (Southern Alleghenies PA), Secretary Melanie Berman (Arkansas), Executive Director Darryl Thomas.
- **Recent transitions:** Idaho APEX in host transition (clients paused); Maine's Miranda Pelkey promoted to Director May 2025; Empire APEX's Lenora Leasure promoted Aug 2024; Montana shows two directors (Langman vs Brownell-interim); MA recompete in 2025 (Simpler.Grants.gov opp 357855).
