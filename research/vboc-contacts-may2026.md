# Nationwide VBOC Contact List (May 2026)
Veterans Business Outreach Centers — SBA cooperative agreement partners.

## Network at a glance
- SBA's current program page states **28 organizations** participate as VBOCs under the current cooperative agreement.
- Cross-referenced directory (UTRGV VBOC site, refreshed within the last 12 months) enumerates **21 distinct lead centers** by coverage area. Gap likely reflects SBA expansion announcements that haven't yet propagated to partner sites.
- Each VBOC is mapped to one of the **10 SBA regions**. Most cover multiple states.
- Common host orgs: universities (ODU, MS State, FSU, UTA, UTRGV, UND, Gulf Coast State), nonprofits (CWE, A New Leaf, Pathway Lending, WWBIC, Big Sky EDA, Business Impact NW).

## Pricing/positioning
- VBOCs are SBA cooperative-agreement-funded, similar OpEx-friendly structure to APEX Accelerators.
- Many VBOCs are co-located with or partnered with SBDCs, Women's Business Centers, or APEX Accelerators at the same host org — cross-sell opportunity.
- Boots to Business (B2B) is the flagship national program; ties VBOCs to DoD TAP installations.
