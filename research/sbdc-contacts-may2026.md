# Nationwide SBDC State Director List (May 2026)
Small Business Development Centers — SBA's largest resource-partner program.

## Network at a glance
- 63 SBDC networks, ~1,000 sub-centers nationwide (America's SBDC 2026 Annual Report).
- 2025–2026: clients secured $5.53B in capital; served nearly 300,000 small businesses.
- Hosted by universities (majority), state economic-development agencies, community colleges.
- This list covers 56 state/territory lead directors (budget-holders) + regional leads for TX (4) and CA (5) = 63 total.

## Why SBDCs matter to MI Enterprise
- Same buyer profile as APEX: federally co-funded; annual budget for scaling tools.
- Many SBDCs run dedicated gov't-contracting tracks — direct cross-sell wedge for MI.
- ~18 state directors are co-located with APEX programs at same host (UAB, UND, UGA/GT, GMU, WSU, UWF, etc.).
