# Nationwide SBDC State Director List (May 2026)
Small Business Development Centers — SBA's largest resource-partner program.

## Network at a glance
- **63 SBDC networks**, ~1,000 sub-centers nationwide (per America's SBDC 2026 Annual Report).
- In 2025–2026 the SBDC network helped clients secure **$5.53 billion in capital** and served nearly **300,000 small businesses**, per America's SBDC.
- Hosted by universities (majority), state economic-development agencies, and community colleges.
- This list contains the **56 state/territory lead directors** — the budget-holders. Each runs a sub-network of 8–25 service centers underneath them.

## Why SBDCs matter to MI Enterprise
- Same buyer profile as APEX: federally-cofunded, services small business clients, has annual budget for tools that scale across staff.
- Many SBDCs run dedicated **government-contracting / federal-procurement tracks** within their state network — that's the direct cross-sell wedge for MI.
- Several state directors are co-located with APEX programs at the same host org (UAB, UND, UGA/Georgia Tech, GMU, Wichita State, UWF, etc.) — flagged below.
- America's SBDC 2025-2026 Board sets national tone. **Byron Hicks (NC SBTDC) is Board Chair** for the second consecutive cycle as of the September 2025 announcement, and **NC SBTDC is the same org that hosts the NC APEX Accelerator in your APEX list** — that's the highest-leverage account in this entire category.
